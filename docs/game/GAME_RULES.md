# StellAdullam Land: Game Rules

Welcome to StellAdullam Land! This is a city-building game where you buy property, earn passive income, and improve buildings to boost profits. Here's everything you need to play.

## The Game Board

The game takes place on a 20×20 grid with 400 properties spread across the virtual city. Each property is a single tile that you can own. When you own a property, it generates income automatically every 8 minutes.

## Getting Started: Your First Properties

When you first join, you receive **1,000 LAND tokens** from the faucet—that's your starting capital.

**Buying from the City**

- The city offers each property for **500 LAND**
- Buy as many as you want using these starter funds
- You own them immediately—no waiting period

**Your Wallet**

- All your money and property ownership is stored in your digital wallet
- You only need to connect once to play

## Earning Income

Every property generates income automatically, even when you're not playing. Here's how it works:

**Income Formula**

- Every 8 minutes, each of your properties earns LAND
- A vacant property earns **10 LAND per cycle**
- The building level on each property multiplies this income:

| Building Level | Cost to Build | Income per Cycle | Total Income |
| -------------- | ------------- | ---------------- | ------------ |
| Vacant         | —             | 10×              | 10 LAND      |
| Residential    | 200 LAND      | 1.5×             | 15 LAND      |
| Commercial     | 600 LAND      | 3×               | 30 LAND      |
| Skyscraper     | 1,800 LAND    | 6×               | 60 LAND      |

**Claiming Your Income**

- Income accumulates over time—it doesn't disappear
- Claim your earned LAND anytime by tapping "Claim" on a property
- After you claim, your income counter resets to 0

## Improving Your Buildings

Want to boost your income? Upgrade your properties to higher building levels.

**How Upgrades Work**

- Tap any property you own
- Select the next building level
- Pay the cost instantly from your LAND balance
- Your property immediately starts earning at the new rate
- You can only upgrade once—each property goes Vacant → Residential → Commercial → Skyscraper

**When Do They Pay Off?**
Most upgrades earn back their cost in 4–8 hours of play. A Skyscraper, for example:

- Costs 2,000 LAND to fully upgrade (200 + 600 + 1,200)
- Earns 60 LAND every 8 minutes (~450 LAND per hour)
- Pays for itself in about 4.5 hours

## Trading on the Marketplace

Want to buy or sell properties from other players? The marketplace lets you trade directly with each other.

**Listing a Property**

1. Tap any property you own
2. Select "List for Sale"
3. Set your price in LAND
4. Other players can now buy it

**Buying from Another Player**

1. Browse the marketplace
2. Find a property you want
3. Tap "Buy Now" and confirm
4. The trade happens instantly—you get the property, the seller gets your LAND

**Why Trade?**

- Upgrade a property, then sell it for profit if you improve it enough
- Buy improved properties cheaply if you find a good deal
- Diversify your portfolio across different city areas

## Strategy Tips

**Start Small**

- Buy a few properties first
- Get comfortable with the income system
- Then plan your expansion

**Diversify**

- Don't put all your LAND into one property
- Spread across the grid for steady income

**Plan Your Upgrades**

- Residential upgrades are cheap and give quick returns
- Commercial is the middle ground
- Save Skyscrapers for your most valuable locations

**Watch the Marketplace**

- Check if underimproved properties are selling cheap
- Buy them, upgrade, and either hold or resell for profit

**Let Your Income Compound**

- Every 8 minutes, your properties earn more LAND
- Use that to buy more properties or upgrades
- Over time, your income grows exponentially

## Game Economy at a Glance

- **Start with:** 1,000 LAND
- **Basic property:** 500 LAND
- **Vacant lot income:** 10 LAND per 8 minutes
- **Skyscraper income:** 60 LAND per 8 minutes (6× multiplier)
- **Income compounds:** Claim rewards, reinvest into upgrades and new properties
- **No reset:** Your buildings stay upgraded forever
